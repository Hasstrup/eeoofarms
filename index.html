What we do

We primarily focus on 3 sustainable species that thrive in Africa, namely; Achatina fulica (The East African land snail), Achatina Achatina (the giant Ghana snail) and Archachatina Marginata (the giant West African snail). These are giant air-breathing tropical land snails that are relatively convenient to farm and most desirable to consumers.
